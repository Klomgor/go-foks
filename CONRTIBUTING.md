## License
By contributing, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the same license as the project, as outlined in the [LICENSE](./LICENSE) file.

## Developer Certificate Of Origin (DCO)

By contributing, you certify that:

- Your contribution was created in whole or in part by you.
- You have the right to submit it under the open source license used by this project.
- You agree to the Developer Certificate of Origin.
- To indicate your agreement, include a line in your commit message such as:
```
Signed-off-by: Your Name <youremail@example.com>
```

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

We currently have no CLA. All contributions are subject to the Developer Certificate of Origin.
